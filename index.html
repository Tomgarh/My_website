Washington, D.C. & the Civil War Battlefields

Itinerary Reviews Included

5 or 8 days

Washington, D.C., Gettysburg, Richmond

Travel back to a time when America was divided by North and South. Gain a deeper understanding of our history as you follow the paths of Civil War soldiers with visits to national parks and battlefields. Then consider how America has evolved as you explore significant sites and monuments in our nation’s capital.

Day 1: Washington, D.C.

Meet your Tour Director at your destination Explore Washington, D.C. highlights. May include:

  • Photo stop at the White House
  • Lafayette Square and Liberty Plaza, former site of the Black Lives Matter Plaza
  • Photo stop at the Washington Monument
  • WWII Memorial
  • Jefferson Memorial & Tidal Basin

    • Visit the Smithsonian museum of your choice, which may include:

  • National Museum of Natural History
  • National Museum of African Art
  • National Museum of American History
  • National Museum of the American Indian
